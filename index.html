About Us

Welcome to S.B. Zircon, a trailblazer in the manufacturing of Zirconium Silicate Opacifier and Zircon Flour. Established in 1989 as S.B. Enterprises, our company has flourished under the visionary leadership of Mr. Shyam Kankani. Today, we stand proud as a leading manufacturer and supplier in the domains of sanitary ware, tiles, investment casting, foundry, and brake linings.

Driven by our unwavering commitment to excellence, we have built a modern manufacturing facility that spans an impressive 70,000 square feet. With a robust production capacity of 1000 MT per month, we ensure a consistent supply of high-quality products to meet the ever-growing demands of our esteemed customers.

In keeping with our motto of "Excellence Always," we have invested in an additional 2,50,000 square feet of land to expand our manufacturing setup. Through this strategic expansion, slated for completion in 2023, we aim to further enhance our capabilities and solidify our position as an industry leader.

At S.B. Zircon, we take immense pride in our relentless pursuit of quality and our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. We strive for perfection in all that we do, consistently exceeding expectations and setting new industry benchmarks.

Join us on our journey as we redefine industry standards and continue to deliver exceptional products and services to our valued customers.
